NEP 2020: Why Coding is Now Essential for School Students

March 1, 2026 Admin News

The National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 has brought a massive paradigm shift in how education is approached in India. Moving away from rote memorization, the policy places a heavy emphasis on 21st-century skills, critical thinking, and digital literacy. One of the most talked-about mandates of this policy is the introduction of coding and computational thinking for school students.

Building a Foundation Early

Starting to learn programming at a young age allows students to absorb the core concepts of logic and problem-solving much faster. At Future Classroom, we have designed a curriculum completely tailored to school students, ensuring that learning to code isn't just about syntax, but about solving real-world problems creatively.

Why It Matters Now More Than Ever

As the digital landscape evolves rapidly with advancements in AI, machine learning, and automation, digital literacy is no longer just a specialized skill reserved for engineers—it is a fundamental life skill. Understanding how technology works beneath the surface helps students become active creators rather than passive consumers.
